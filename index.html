About

My name is Edmond Nzivugira, and I first touched a digital camera in my freshman year at Colgate University. A lot of things happen in life, and it's only a few we can remember. Taking pictures helps me record the moments that matter; those little details that often go unnoticed.

Based in Hamilton, New York. Available for commissions, editorials, and collaborations.
